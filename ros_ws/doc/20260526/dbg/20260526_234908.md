## Official Launch Behavior

`turtlebot4_gz.launch.py` passes a short `world` name to `sim.launch.py`.

`sim.launch.py` resolves the world through `GZ_SIM_RESOURCE_PATH` and appends `.sdf`.

Implication:

- Do not edit official launch files.
- Local launch should expose `src/sim/worlds` to Gazebo or directly pass the custom world path.
